When will I see results? +
Most customers see visible improvement in brightness and texture within 7–10 days. For significant reduction in dark spots and improved firmness, use consistently for 28 days. Our formula is designed to work for all skin types.
Is this suitable for sensitive skin? +
Yes. Our formula is dermatologically tested and formulated to be gentle on all skin types including sensitive and acne-prone skin. We recommend a patch test first if you have a known skin condition.
How do I use the serum? +
Apply 2–3 drops to clean, dry skin morning and evening. Gently press into your skin with upward motions, then follow with moisturiser and SPF in the morning. Consistent daily use gives the best results.
